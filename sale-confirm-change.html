Happy {day_of_week}! Today we’re making an important change to the Tradesy sale confirmation policy: From now on, you’ll be required to confirm all sales within 3 days, instead of 5.

Why are we doing this? Glad you asked! Tradesy buyers want to be promptly informed on those rare occasions when a seller needs to cancel an order. Well-informed buyers are happier and purchase more often, which makes Tradesy work better for all of us.

What you need to know:
• All of your sales will now need to be confirmed within 3 days, otherwise they’ll be automatically cancelled and the buyer will be notified and refunded.
• Any sale that occurred before today will still have 5 days to be confirmed.
• Faster sale confirmation & shipping means you get paid sooner.
If you have questions about how to confirm a sale or about our new policy, our help center article about sale confirmations is your new best friend, and we’re here to help if you need it.
